Background

Youth Initiatives for Development and Human Rights Organization (YiDHO)

is Non-Governmental Organization, formally registered with the Rwanda Governance Board (Registration N0 822/RGB/NGO/OC/12/2020). YiDHO founded in 2018 by the youthtrained by AJPRODHO-JIJUKIRWA about human rights in partnership with GIZ Development cooperation. YiDHOis implementing the Integrated Coaching on Human and Sexual Reproductive Rights to Rwandan young educates (iCARE) project.

Mission

Empowering youth to attain peaceful and sustainable development through human rights awareness’ .

Vision

We envision to have youth advocates of human rights and peaceful development.

Location

Kigali city-Gasabo-Remera.
